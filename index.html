Clinical Steps

Clinical RLE

My duties during my Related Learning Experience (RLE) involve a combination of classroom learning and hands-on clinical practice. In the clinical setting, I work alongside experienced nurses and healthcare professionals to apply theoretical knowledge to real-life patient care situations. My responsibilities include tasks such as taking vital signs, administering medications, assisting with patient assessments, and providing basic nursing care under supervision. Additionally, I actively participate in healthcare team discussions, contribute to care planning, and engage in reflective practices to enhance my learning and develop essential clinical skills. Overall, my RLE duty is a crucial part of my education, allowing me to integrate theoretical concepts into practical, patient-centered experiences.

Community Health Nurse RLE

As a community health nurse in Barangay Tabid, my role encompasses a variety of responsibilities aimed at promoting health and well-being within the community. I collaborate with local health officials, community leaders, and residents to assess the healthcare needs of the population. My duties involve conducting health assessments, organizing health education programs, and providing preventive care services. I work closely with individuals and families, addressing issues such as immunizations, maternal and child health, and communicable disease prevention. Additionally, I engage in community outreach initiatives to raise awareness about health issues and encourage active participation in maintaining a healthy lifestyle. By fostering strong relationships within Barangay Tabid, I aim to empower the community to take ownership of their health and work collaboratively toward achieving optimal well-being.

School setting

As a student officer in school, my role is to serve as a representative and advocate for the student body, ensuring their voices are heard and needs addressed. I actively collaborate with fellow officers, faculty, and administrators to organize and execute events that enhance the student experience and foster a sense of community. Additionally, I take on responsibilities related to student welfare, such as addressing concerns, facilitating communication, and promoting a positive and inclusive learning environment. Through effective leadership and teamwork, I strive to contribute to the overall well-being and success of my peers, creating a vibrant and supportive academic community.
